There's One in Every Family is the second  studio album released by American  rapper, Fiend. It was released on May 5, 1998 on No Limit Records and was produced by Master P and Beats By the Pound. Like most of the albums released by No Limit in the late 1990s, the album was a success, peaking at #8 on the Billboard 200 and #1 on the Top R&B/Hip-Hop Albums selling 222,000 copies in its first week, and featured most of the labels top acts, such as Master P, Snoop Dogg, Silkk the Shocker and C-Murder.
