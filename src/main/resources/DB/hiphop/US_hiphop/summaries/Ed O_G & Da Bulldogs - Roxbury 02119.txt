Roxbury 02119 is the second studio album by Boston-based rapper Ed O.G. & Da Bulldogs. It was released via Chemistry Records/Mercury Records/PolyGram in 1993. The album peaked at number 36 on the Top R&B/Hip-Hop Albums. The album spawned two singles: "Skinny Dip (Got It Goin' On)", which peaked at number 19 on the Hot Rap Songs, and "Love Comes & Goes", which peaked at number 43 on the Hot Rap Songs.
