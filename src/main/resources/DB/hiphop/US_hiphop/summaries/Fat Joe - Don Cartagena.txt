Don Cartagena is the third studio album by American rapper Fat Joe. The album was released on September 1, 1998, by Terror Squad, Mystic, Big Beat and Atlantic Records. Don Cartagena debuted at #7 on the Billboard 200, selling 106,000 copies in the first week.The album was Gold by the Recording Industry Association of America. Official music videos were done for "John Blaze", "Don Cartagena", and "Bet Ya Man Can't (Triz)"
