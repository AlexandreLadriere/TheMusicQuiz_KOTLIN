Télétoon Rétro, stylized in TELETOON Rétro, was a specialized Quebec television channel category B owned by Corus Entertainment, based on Télétoon's former Télétoon Rétro programming block.
The program offered classic cartoons whose broadcast on Télétoon has stopped for more than a decade. An identical service in the English language, Teletoon Retro, had been on the air since October 1, 2007.
