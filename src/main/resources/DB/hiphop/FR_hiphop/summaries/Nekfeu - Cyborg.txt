Cyborg is the second studio album of French rapper Nekfeu released on December 2, 2016 on the label Seine Zoo. He is currently certified platinum triple disc.
Announced exceptionally, during a concert at the AccorHotelArena (Bercy) in December 2016, as a surprise album, Cyborg receives a very good commercial reception, with currently more than 400,000 copies sold without any advertising, which is worth platinum triple disc certification.
It breaks the record of the most streamed French album in 24 hours on the Spotify platform. He also receives a very critical welcome from the first days following his release, both from the public and the media.
