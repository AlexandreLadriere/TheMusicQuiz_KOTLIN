He is the precursor of the "Afro trap", a mix of African sounds and trap music. In 2012, he began his career in the band 1.9 Networks, formed with friends from his neighborhood. In summer 2015, he stands out thanks to a "freestyle" rap, posted on social networks. The video very quickly meets the success of Internet users and arouses the craze of the media. On April 15, 2016, he released his first solo album MHD, which launched the rapper's career, certified platinum double disc in France. On September 19, 2018, he publishes his second album titled 19, certified gold record. He announces at the same time want to end his career after the release of this latest album. By the end of 2018, his clips have nearly 800 million views on YouTube.
On January 15, 2019, he is taken into custody in the context of an investigation into the murder of a 23-year-old man who was attacked by a dozen individuals during the night of July 5 to 6, 2018 in Paris. . On 17 January 2019, he is indicted for murder and placed in pre-trial detention.
