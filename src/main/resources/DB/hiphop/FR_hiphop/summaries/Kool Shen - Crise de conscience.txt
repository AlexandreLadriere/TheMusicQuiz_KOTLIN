Kool Shen, real name Bruno Lopes, born February 9, 1966 in Saint-Denis (Seine-Saint-Denis), is a rapper, producer, breaker, graffiti artist, actor, and French poker player. He is a co-founder of the Supreme NTM rap group with JoeyStarr, and is one of the major figures of French rap. In 2004, he published his first solo album entitled Dernier Derniere. In addition to his musical career, Kool Shen is also dedicated to high-level poker.
