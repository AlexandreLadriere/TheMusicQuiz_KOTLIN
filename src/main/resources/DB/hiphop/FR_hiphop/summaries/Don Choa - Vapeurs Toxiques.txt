Don Choa, whose real name is François Dilhan, born August 1, 1974 in Toulouse, Haute-Garonne, is a French rapper. He is one of the four MCs of the Fonky Family rap group in Marseille. Don Choa published his first solo album, Toxic Vapors, in November 2002, followed by Concrete Jungle in October 2007. His stage name Choa is the Provençal Occitan diminutive of the first name François.
