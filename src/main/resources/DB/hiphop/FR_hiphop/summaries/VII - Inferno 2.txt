Masakazu Morita (森田 成 一, Morita Masakazu) (born October 12, 1973) is a Japanese voice dubber and actor born in Tokyo, Japan. He is currently working for Aoni Production, and he hosts the radio show Bleach B-Station. Morita is best known for dubbing Ichigo Kurosaki (Bleach), Tidus (Final Fantasy X), Auel Nieder (Gundam Seed Destiny), Whis (Dragon Ball Z: Battle of Gods, Dragon Ball Super) and Seiya (Saint Seiya). Hades: Chapter Inferno).
According to Tite Kubo, the author of Bleach, Morita is extremely energetic, very friendly and open.
