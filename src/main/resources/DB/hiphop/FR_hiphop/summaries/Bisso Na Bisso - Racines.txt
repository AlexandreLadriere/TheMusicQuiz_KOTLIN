Bisso Na Bisso is a Franco-Congolese hip hop collective formed in 1997. Bísó na bísó means "between us" in Lingala.
On their two albums, the group collaborates with great African musicians and singers from Congo-Brazzaville, then from Senegal and Ivory Coast, or Antilles (France and Cuba): Monique Séka, Ismaël Lo, Lokua Kanza, Papa Wemba, Koffi Olomide, Tanya St. Val, Jacob Desvarieux of Kassav ', Manu Dibango, Roldan Gonzalez Rivero of the group Orishas, ​​and Khaled.
