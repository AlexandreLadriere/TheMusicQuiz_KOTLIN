Voluntary Homicide is the second album of rap group Assassin, released June 2, 1995. It received a gold record in 1996. This album is often considered [By whom?] As The best of Assassin.
Recorded and mixed in Los Angeles, the album is marked by the various influences of the group (soul, funk, ragga ...), as well as by the production of Doctor L. The band will go on tour with the group Kabal that their label also produces .
