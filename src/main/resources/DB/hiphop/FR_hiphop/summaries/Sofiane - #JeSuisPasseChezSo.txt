Sofiane, also known as Fianso, by her full name Sofiane Zermani, born July 21, 1986 in Saint-Denis (Seine-Saint-Denis) in France, is a French rapper from Blanc-Mesnil.
Since September 8, 2017, Sofiane presents Rentre dans le Cercle, a series of videos where he invites rappers to perform in turn and representatives of hip-hop and record labels to answer questions about their professions.
