The Women's Football World Cup 2011 is the sixth edition of the Women's Football World Cup and took place from June 26 to July 17, 2011 in Germany. This is the second World Cup finals on the European continent. The organizing country was chosen in October 2007 by the International Federation of Football Association (FIFA).
The national teams of 126 countries are participating in a qualifying phase in 2010, with the aim of selecting the 15 teams competing in the final tournament with the German team, which qualifies as the organizing country.
Japan wins its first world championship title by beating the United States in the final and becomes the first Asian team to win in the competition.
