Assassin is an independent French hip-hop band. It was formed in 1985 by Rockin 'Squat and Solo, quickly joined by DJ Clyde then Doctor L. Since 2000, the tours are made with Rockin' Squat, Profecy, Lyricson and DJ Duke.
The group fights the media and political system, through music. He often refers to the roots of rap by reminding his audience of the fundamentals of hip-hop culture: he rejects all kinds of sectarianism and calls for respect, love, unity, fighting spirit and education.
Assassin has three gold albums for the album The Future that we reserve, The Voluntary Homicide and Touche d'espoir with more than 100,000 album sold for each of them.
