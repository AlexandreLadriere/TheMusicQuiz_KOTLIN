Deo Favente is the second studio album of the French rapper SCH, it was released on May 5, 2017 under the labels Braabus Music, Millenium / Capitol, Universal. Deo Favente means "with the favor of God" and is now certified platinum with over 160,000 sales. At the singles level, 6.45i is certified single platinum. The pieces Mac 11, Russian Doll and As if are themselves certified single gold.
