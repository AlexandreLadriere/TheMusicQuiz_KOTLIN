Akhenaton (pronounced: a.kenɑːtɔn), whose real name is Philippe Fragione, born September 17, 1968 in Marseille, is a rapper, producer, director and host of French radio.
As an emblematic member of the IAM group, Akhenaton is, according to RFI, "one of the most listened representatives of rap in France. His handling of figures of speech and rhyme, his extensive experience in instrumental production and his talents as a screenwriter have earned him artistic recognition that extends beyond the French rap scene in which he is one of most influential.
