Milk Coffee and Sugar is a French hip-hop and slam band from Paris. Formed in 2008, the group consists of rappers Gael Faye and Edgar Sekloka aka Suga. They are mainly influenced by the American jazz rap group The Roots, the French hip-hop collective Time Bomb and African music. Surrounded by beatmakers Jr EakEe, Fabiolitto, Kumotronic or Boolban, the band is accompanied on stage by the composer and arranger Guillaume Poncelet who assures trumpet and keyboard, as well as the drummer of Electro Deluxe, Arnaud Renaville.
