Princess Lalla Salma (in Amazigh: ⵍⴰⵍⵍⴰ ⵙⵍⵎⴰ, in Arabic: للا سلمى), born Salma Bennani, on May 10, 1978 in Fes, is the wife of King Mohammed VI of Morocco and the mother of Crown Prince Moulay El Hassan and Princess Lalla Khadija. She is de facto the first lady of Morocco and bears the title of princess with qualification of royal highness. However, it has not been seen in public since the end of 2017.
