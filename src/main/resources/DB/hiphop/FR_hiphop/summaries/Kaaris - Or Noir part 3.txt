Kaaris, stylized KΛΛRIS, his real name Okou Armand Gnakouri, born January 30, 1980 in Cocody, Abidjan, is a French rapper, composer, actor, producer and entrepreneur of Ivorian origin. In 2013, he published Black Gold produced by Therapy Music. The album knows a real success which led Kaaris to reissue it (Black Gold Part 2, classic and deluxe editions). He is considered as the artist having popularized the trap in France.
