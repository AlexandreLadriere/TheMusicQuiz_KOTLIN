Les Anges, formerly Les Anges de la téléréalité, is a French reality show produced by La Grosse Équipe and broadcast on NRJ 12 since January 10, 2011.
The principle is to coexist former candidates of reality shows in a villa located in a city or a mythical region of a foreign country, which varies each season. The goal of the candidates is to break into their professional circles thanks to the many contacts of the godfather of the Angels.
The credits of the show is a featuring between Gilles Luka and singer Nyusha. The title is called Nearer (We Can Make It Right). During the eighth season, the candidate Aurélie Preston realized, with Gilles Luka, a new version of the same title which then became the credits of the season.
The show is broadcast Monday to Friday in the early evening. It begins with The Mad Mag, a show on stage bringing together personalities of reality TV with chronicles, followed by the episode of the day.
The first season ended on February 17, 2011. Following the success of this first edition, a second season was broadcast from May 23, 2011 to June 23, 2011 and a third from September 26, 2011 to November 25, 2011. The fourth The season started on April 16, 2012 and ended on June 29, 2012. The fifth season was broadcast on Sunday, March 3, 2013 with a bonus in the second half of the evening and ended on July 2, 2013 with a late bonus. The sixth season was aired from March 9, 2014 and ended on July 6, 2014 with a new end bonus. The seventh season was aired March 8, 2015 and ended July 5, 2015 on a premium. The eighth season was broadcast from February 22, 2016 to July 1, 2016. Following the departure of Matthieu Delormeau from NRJ 12, it is Ayem Nour who becomes host of the show. The ninth season was broadcast from February 6, 2017 to June 30, 2017. The tenth season was broadcast between March 12, 2018 and July 6, 2018. Finally, the eleventh season was broadcast from February 4, 2019 to July 5, 2019
