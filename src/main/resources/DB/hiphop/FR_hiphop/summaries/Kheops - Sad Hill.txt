Kheops, whose real name Eric Mazel, born May 4, 1966 in Marseille, is a disc jockey and producer of French hip-hop. He founded in 1986, alongside the rapper Akhenaton the mythical French rap group IAM. Kheops runs his own record label, Sad Hill Records, which he launches in 1997. He is also known for being a great collector of vinyl records: his collection has more than 20,000 records.
