Edens Zero (ens デ ン ズ ゼ ロ, Edensu Zero) is a shōnen manga written and drawn by Hiro Mashima. It has been prepublished in Kōdansha's Weekly Shōnen Magazine since June 27, 2018. Pika Publishing publishes simultaneously the French version of each chapter simultaneously and will release the first volume bound on October 10, 2018.
Edens Zero is the new manga of Hiro Mashima, released about a year after its flagship series Fairy Tail, whose design of some characters are reminiscent of this previous manga.
