Ateyaba, formerly Joke, whose real name is Gilles Ateyaba Koffi Soler, born October 27, 1989 in Narbonne in the Aude, is a French rapper from Montpellier. After the release of the EP Kyoto, November 26, 2011, and a full concert at La Maroquinerie de Paris in December, he is considered in 2012 as a promising talent for French rap. In January 2015, he published a free EP called DeLorean Music. Finally on March 27, 2017, on the occasion of the Air Max Day for the Air Vapor Max, he released an extract of his future album "Ultraviolet" named "Vision" in collaboration with Nike. He initially announced this album for November 2017, before postponing its release until 2019.
