Sya Styles, whose real name is Rachid Aït Baar, born April 25, 1978 in Marseille and died October 26, 2015 in the same city, was a French DJ / producer of Moroccan origin.
He is a DJ of the group Psy 4 De La Rime, which he formed with his childhood friends, the three cousins ​​Segnor Alonzo, Don Vincenzo and Soprano, from the Comoros and member of the "Suck me Klub".
