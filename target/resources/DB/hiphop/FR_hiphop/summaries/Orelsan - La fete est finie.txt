The party is over is the third studio album of the French rapper Orelsan, released in 2017 on the labels 3e bureau and Wagram. The album knows a success as commercial as critical.
The party is over ranks at the top of the charts French and Walloon and is certified gold record in three days. The album is awarded at Victoires de la musique in the category of the best album of urban music. In June 2018, he is certified diamond disc.
November 15, 2018 released a reissue to the album, Epilogue, adding 11 more titles.
