Romeo Elvis, whose real name is Romeo Johnny Elvis Kiki Van Laeken, born December 13, 1992 in Uccle, is a Belgian rapper.
After the release of two EPs released respectively in 2013 and 2014: Brussels became the jungle and Large family, he publishes in 2016 Morale EP that launches the rapper's career followed by his hit album Morale 2 in 2017, which allows him to make himself known to the general public, reissued Morale 2luxe in 2018, from his collaboration with the producer Le Motel. His second album, and first solo, Chocolat was released on April 12, 2019.
