Hocus Pocus is a French hip-hop band from Nantes, Loire-Atlantique. Formed in 1995, the group is primarily influenced by the American rap scene of the 1990s such as A Tribe Called Quest, Mos Def, Jay Dee, The Roots, Jurassic 5 and Pete Rock [ref. desired].
The band currently consists of 20Syl, DJ Greem, David Le Deunff (guitar and vocals), Hervé Godard (bass guitar), Matthieu Lelièvre (piano), Antoine Saint-Jean (drums and brass sometimes). 20Syl and Greem are also members of the group of DJs C2C, ex Coups2Cross, quadruple world champion DMC.
