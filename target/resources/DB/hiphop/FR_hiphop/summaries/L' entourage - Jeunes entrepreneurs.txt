Entourage is a French hip-hop collective from Paris. Formed in 2008, it is composed of eleven rappers: Alpha Wann, Deen Burbigo, Doums, Eff Gee, Fonky Flav, Framal, Jazzy Bazz, Mekra, LuXe, Nekfeu and 2zer. The collective also has other members who do not like Candy Cotton, Neefa, Marguerite Du Bled, Abu, etc. They have a studio album, Jeunes Entrepreneurs, published in 2014.
