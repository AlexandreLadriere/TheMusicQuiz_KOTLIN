Regain de tension is a rap album from the group La Rumeur, released on October 18, 2004 in France, and received a rather positive reception from the music critic. The piece We're the first on ... is notably known to be a virulent critic of the Skyrock radio, considered by La Rumeur as having led to a commercial drift of rap music at the expense of the authenticity of the claims and the commitment political, this song was first released on the EP of the same name in 2003 accompanied by 4 other titles.
