Dubmatique is a Canadian hip-hop group from Montreal, Quebec. He was trained in 1992 by Jérôme-Philippe Bélinga (Disoul), Ousmane Traoré (OTMC) and Alain Benabdallah (DJ Choice). Dubmatique is often considered one of the pillars of Quebec rap, having opened the door to a new generation of artists. In this category, he is the only group to have a platinum-certified record.
