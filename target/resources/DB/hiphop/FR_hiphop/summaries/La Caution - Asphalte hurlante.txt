The Caution is a French hip-hop band from Noisy-le-Sec, Seine-Saint-Denis. Formed in the mid-1990s, it consists of Hi-Tekk and Nikkfurie, supported by DJ Fab. In parallel, Hi-Tekk and Nikkfurie are part of the collective Cautionneurs, with Saphir the jeweler, Izno (the little brother of Hi-Tekk and Nikk) and 16S64.
