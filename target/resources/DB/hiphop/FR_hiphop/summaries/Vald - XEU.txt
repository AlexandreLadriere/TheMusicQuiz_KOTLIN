Vald, pseudonym of Valentin Le Du, born July 15, 1992 and from Aulnay-sous-Bois in Seine-Saint-Denis, is a French rapper.
After two mixtapes and two EPs, he released his first studio album called Agartha, now platinum disc, on January 20, 2017. It will be followed by XEU, released on February 2, 2018, which has a very good start: more 40,000 sales in the first week and a gold certification ten days after its release. On March 9, 2018, just over a month after its release, XEU has over 100,000 sales or streams, certifying platinum record.
