Shay (pronounced: [Êƒaj]), whose real name is Vanessa Lesnicki, born on 16 August 1992 in Brussels [ref. necessary], is a Belgian rapper of Polish and Congolese origin.
After a collaboration with Booba in 2011, she was revealed to the general public by her PMW title in the summer of 2016. She released her first album Jolie Garce on December 2, 2016 which will be certified gold.
She released her second album on May 10, 2019, named Antidote.
