The discography of French singer Jul is composed of twelve studio albums and six mixtapes. It also includes twenty-nine singles.
During his career, Jul earned twelve gold records, ten platinum records, six platinum doubles, three platinum triple discs and one diamond disc. He also got twenty-two gold singles, seven platinum singles and two diamond singles.
Jul has sold more than 2.6 million albums since the beginning of his career and currently ranks third in the sales rankings in the history of French rap, before him is MC Solaar and the group IAM.
