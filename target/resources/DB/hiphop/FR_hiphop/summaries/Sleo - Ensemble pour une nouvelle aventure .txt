Lady Laistee, whose real name is Aline Farran, born April 20, 1972 in Saint-Claude, Guadeloupe, is a French rapper. She embarked on rap in the early 1990s in the group The Plot Bas fonds. In 1999, she released her first solo album, Black Mama, on which she paid homage to her brother, Rudy, shot three years earlier in 1996 on the song Et si ... ?. In 2001, Aline released her second album, Hip-Hop Therapy, which was followed in 2005 by her third album, Second Wind.
