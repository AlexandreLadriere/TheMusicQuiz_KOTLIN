Chapter 7, the seventh album by MC Solaar (4 years after Mach 6) is a logical continuation of the previous album already inspired by his travels, this time in New York. This album was released on June 18, 2007.
The first single is Da Vinci Claude followed by Clic Clic and Carpe Diem. 59,000 sales for the first six weeks of grading and 150,000 in five months (double gold).
This album was awarded at the 2008 Victoires de la musique in the urban music album category of the year among T'as vu (Fatal Bazooka), Place 54 (Hocus Pocus), Season 5 (IAM).
