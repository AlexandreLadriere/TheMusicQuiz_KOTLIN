Disiz [di.siz], or Disiz la Plague, by his real name Sérigne M'Baye Gueye, born March 22, 1978 in Amiens, in the Somme, is a French rapper, writer and comedian. He started in the Rimeurs Gages group. He released his first album Le Poisson rouge, on October 23, 2000, which contains the single J'pète les plombs inspired by the film Joel Schumacher's Falling Down, released in the summer of 2000, a great success on sale with 500,000 singles sold.
At the beginning of 2005, Disiz is showing Denis Thybaud's film Dans vos rêves, where he plays Ixe, a young rapper who seeks to fulfill his dream. In 2009, he announced the end of his rapper career; in an interview with Streetlive Magazine, Disiz expresses his wish to direct his musical career towards rock and also expresses his fears about this choice. He returns on March 8, 2012 with a second novel, René, and on March 26 with a Lucide EP, first part of the Lucide trilogy
