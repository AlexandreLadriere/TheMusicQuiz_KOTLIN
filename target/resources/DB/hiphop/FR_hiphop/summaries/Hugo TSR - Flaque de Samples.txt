Hugo TSR, born January 18, 1985 in Paris, is an independent French rapper and graffiti artist. He started in rap in 2000 with TSR crew. Singer-songwriter, he assumes the entire music production chain: recording, mixing, webmastering and computer graphics. He made himself known thanks to his first album published in 2005, entitled La Bomb H but mainly thanks to the TSR Crew.
While we are there and Window Street are the most famous albums but it offers more confidential productions such as Puddle Samples, The Waiting Room and Bomb H.
