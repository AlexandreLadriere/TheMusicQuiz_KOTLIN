Columbine is a French hip-hop and rap collective from the suburbs of Rennes, Brittany, worn by Lujipeka, whose real name is Lucas Taupin and Foda C, whose real name is Theo Hiribarne. The collective was formed in 2014.
The group has been very successful: 95 sold-out dates since 2017, a first Platinum (Terrible Children's) record album, more than 150 million streams. After 2 Olympia sold out, their third album Adieu soon is certified platinum.
