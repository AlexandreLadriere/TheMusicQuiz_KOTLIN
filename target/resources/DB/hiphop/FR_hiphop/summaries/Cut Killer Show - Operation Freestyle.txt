Cut Killer (pronounced: [kʌt kɪlə (ɹ)]), whose real name is Anouar Hajoui, born May 6, 1971 in Meknes, Morocco, is a French DJ of Moroccan origin. He has hosted his own radio show Cutkiller Show for about fifteen years on Skyrock, and has included the DJ Big Dawg Pitbulls Funkmaster Flex, and Shadyville DJ's 50 Cent.
