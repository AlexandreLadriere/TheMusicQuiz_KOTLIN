Atach Tatuq is a Canadian hip-hop band from Montreal, Quebec. The band formed in 1998 under the original name of Traumaturges, and published a first album called Suck my index. Friends complete the group and the group is renamed Atach Tatuq. In 2003, the group published The War of the Tuqs. He finished in 2005 with the hit album Deluxxx, after which the band dissolves.
