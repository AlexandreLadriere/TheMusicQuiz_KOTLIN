Menelik, whose real name is Albert Tjamag, born September 10, 1970 in Yaoundé, Cameroon, is a French rapper and audiovisual producer. Ménélik publishes his first album Phénénélik in 1995 with the DJ's producers DJ Seeq and Logilo at Jimmy Jay Records which places him in license at S.M.A.L.L., label of Sony Music. He stops his musical career in 2010 to devote himself to the audiovisual production, before making his return with a new album in 2017.
